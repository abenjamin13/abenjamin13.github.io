History of Chapman AEÎ 

The Alpha Omega Chapter of the Alpha Epsilon Pi fraternity at Chapman University was established in the Fall semester of 2011 as a nationally recognized colony. After 6 semesters as a registered Chapman University interest group, Alpha Epsilon Pi of Chapman University has gained official status as a member of the Inter Fraternity Council at Chapman University. We are looking forward to the coming semester and all our new opportunities.
